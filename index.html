About

Hello, I'm Mohammad
a UI/UX Designer based in Damascus.

With over 5 years of experience, I design at the intersection of craft and strategy — creating UI systems, interaction flows, and digital products that are as thoughtful as they are functional. I've worked remotely with companies across Saudi Arabia, Turkey, UAE, Syria, and Germany.

My background spans the full design spectrum: UX research, product design, motion graphics, brand identity, and front-end development. This means I understand how design decisions ripple from a wireframe to deployed code — and I care deeply about every step.

Currently open to freelance projects and full-time roles worldwide. Senior-level thinking, agency speed, bias toward outcomes.
